About Me

I am a PhD student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China, advised by Prof. Hefei Lin. I work on deep learning, computer vision and visual scurity, focusing on fake face detection. Before that, I did my bachelor's degree at School of Mathematics and Statistics, HUST, and master's degree at School of Computer Science and Technology, HUST.
